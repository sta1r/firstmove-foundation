Please note

Any opportunities advertised on the LCF Careers Live website that are unpaid and not part of accredited short term work experience or a long term work placement will be removed immediately by the LCF Careers team.

All other opportunities must be paid at a rate of at least National Minimum Wage (NMW). For more information on the current NMW rate please go to - https://www.gov.uk/national-minimum-wage-rates. By registering you are agreeing to cooperate with these guidelines.
